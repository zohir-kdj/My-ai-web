Privacy Policy

At Expert AI, accessible from https://expertai.eu.cc, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Expert AI and how we use it.

Log Files

Expert AI follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Expert AI uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.
